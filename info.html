LOCATION

The wedding will take place at Albright Hussey Manor, Ellesmere Road, Shrewsbury, SY4 3AF on DATE/TIME. A stately 16th century Tudor country house, the manor is now an upmarket hotel not far from Shrewsbury Abbey and Attingham Park mansion. Offering both standard nightly accomodation and a variety of events, the manor has a reputation for quality occasions, weddings included! Sam and Donna believe it to be the perfect location that will reflect both their desire for each other as well as a damn good knees up.

DIRECTIONS

Please see the below google maps location for the manor. For anyone travelling from Shrewsbury, simply head out of town on the A528, straight over the mini roundabout, then continue on to the next roundabout. Straight over once again, then take your first right. Alternatively, stick SY4 3AF into your Sat-Nav or grab a paper map.
